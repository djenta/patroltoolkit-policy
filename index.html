# Privacy Policy for Patrol Toolkit Effective Date: April 10, 2026 Last Updated: April 22, 2026 Patrol Toolkit is operated by Keyat Packle LLC ("Company", "we", "our", "us"). Patrol Toolkit is a local-first mobile application for law enforcement professionals. This policy explains what the app accesses, how that data is stored, and what purchase-related information is handled by third parties. ## 1. Information We Do Not Collect We do not operate servers that store your Patrol Toolkit content. The following remain on your device unless you explicitly export or share them: - Case notes, call numbers, and incident details - Evidence photos captured in the app - Department documents you import - Calendar entries, court dates, and reminders - Pursuit routes, waypoints, and notes - SFST results and observations - Local feature preferences and app lock state We do not collect your name, email address, contact list, or payment card details through the app itself. ## 2. Device Permissions Patrol Toolkit requests only the permissions needed for specific features: - Camera: used to capture evidence photos and support the SFST flashlight workflow - Face ID / biometric authentication: used to lock and unlock access to locally stored app data If you deny a permission, the related feature may be limited, but the app remains free to install and use for supported non-permission features. ## 3. Purchases and Billing Patrol Toolkit is free to install. SFST Timer and Law Reference are available at no cost. Paid features are unlocked through a one-time $9.99 lifetime unlock purchased via Apple App Store or Google Play. We do not directly process payments. Billing and refunds are handled by Apple or Google under their own policies. We use RevenueCat to validate whether a lifetime unlock is active for the current store account. RevenueCat receives only the minimum transaction data needed to verify purchase status, such as anonymous app user identifiers and store receipt information. ## 4. Third-Party Services Patrol Toolkit relies on the following third-party services: - Apple App Store / Google Play Store for app distribution and in-app purchases - RevenueCat for purchase status verification - Apple Maps / Google Maps and OpenStreetMap routing providers for pursuit mapping features Your interaction with those services is governed by their respective privacy policies. ## 5. Data Storage and Security User-generated operational data is intended to remain local to your device. Patrol Toolkit uses encrypted local database storage and the device secure enclave / keychain where supported for sensitive local secrets. If you export or share data using platform features, that exported content is then handled according to the destination you choose. ## 6. Retention and Deletion Because your Patrol Toolkit data is stored locally: - You can delete individual records from within the app - Uninstalling the app removes app-managed local data from the device, subject to platform backup behavior - There is no server-side account data with us to delete ## 7. Children's Privacy Patrol Toolkit is not directed to children and is intended for professional adult use. ## 8.
